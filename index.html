About

The Berkeley Project aims to permanently change the relationship between the students and residents of Berkeley through hands-on and virtual community service. The Berkeley Project hosts one large-scale community service even every semester.

Berkeley Project Day is a 2,000 person event where volunteers work on service projects throughout the City of Berkeley. From working at elementary schools to planting trees, our volunteers devote a total of 12,000+ hours and save the City of Berkeley over $400,000 every year.
